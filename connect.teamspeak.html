Step One

Download the Teamspeak software here and install it, after that is done run the program.

Step Two

Once the program opens you should see something similar to the following picture.

Step Three

Click on "Connections" followed by "Connect".

Step four

A new window will open up, in it fill the "Server Adress:" with "www.hexbin.net",
in the "Nickname:" choose one that you like
finaly in the "Server Password:" fill it with "CHALON" without the quotations marks.

Step five

Press the connect button and that's it!
